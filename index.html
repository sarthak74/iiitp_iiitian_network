    IIIT Pune


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information technology, Pune, Karnataka is one of the new IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India.IIIT pune has initiated its functioning in Hyderabad in August 2019, under the mentorship of IIT Hyderabad. IIIT pune has started its first batch in the academic year 2019-20 with a strength of 30 students in the department of computer science and engineering.

    STUDENT LIFE

    The students have started several clubs under the mentorship of IIT Hyderabad and are learning a lot of skills from their IITH seniors. All the students are actively participating in the Technical and cultural activities. Elan & Nvision the annual Techno cultural fest, E-Cell IITH, Milan GC, TEDx IITH are some activities in which the students are deeply involved and developing their skills.

    NEWS & UPDATES

    A research authored by Rohan Lekhwani and Bhupendra Singh has been accepted for presentation in ICICC-2020.

    A research paper authored by Rohan Lekhwani and Bhupendra Singh on "FastV2C-HandNet : Fast Voxel to Coordinate Hand Pose Estimation with 3D ConvNets" has been accepted for presentation in International Conference on Innovative Computing and Communications (ICICC-2020) to be held at University of Delhi, New Delhi from February 21-23, 2020.

    Project Sahara

    Project Sahara is an outcome of one such effort by a group of students who aim to raise funds to help the underprivileged by organising online events like Treasure Hunt, Quiz and eSports.

    Republic Day Celebration

    IIIT Pune celebrated RepublicDay with Dr. Anupam Shukla, Director, IIIT Pune, hoisting the National Flag. The Students performed various programs such as drama, singing, band performances, a special blood donation camp was also organized by the IIITP.

    EBSB: A visit to IIIT Trichy

    Under EBSB program student echange program organised between IIIT Pune and IIIT Trichy. IIIT Pune students visited Trichy in Jan 2020 and IIIT Trichy students visited Pune in Feb 2020.
