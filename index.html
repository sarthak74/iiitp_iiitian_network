    IIIT Pune


    FOUNDATION

    The Indian Institute of Information technology, Pune, Maharastra is one of the new IIITs proposed by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India under PPP mode with companies Rolta & TownHub as its partners.

    STUDENT LIFE

    IIITP follows international curriculum and course it provide is up to date not like any other IIITs and IITs. And encourage students to think out of the box. It has several clubs that help the students to grow their domain in the fields of Tech, Art, Culture and Sportsmanship. Having the young minds to be prepared to take up social challenges,inculcating them with leadership skills and entreprenuer skills.

    Computer Science Engineering

    The Institute provides all the three degrees B.Tech , M.Tech and PhD under department of Computer Science. The first of B.Tech started in 2016 , whereas M.Tech and PhD batches started in 2019.

    Electronics and Communication Engineering

    The Institute provides all the three degrees B.Tech , M.Tech and PhD under department of Electronics and Communication. The first of B.Tech started in 2016 , whereas M.Tech and PhD batches started in 2019.

    NEWS & UPDATES

    A research authored by Rohan Lekhwani and Bhupendra Singh has been accepted for presentation in ICICC-2020.

    A research paper authored by Rohan Lekhwani and Bhupendra Singh on "FastV2C-HandNet : Fast Voxel to Coordinate Hand Pose Estimation with 3D ConvNets" has been accepted for presentation in International Conference on Innovative Computing and Communications (ICICC-2020) to be held at University of Delhi, New Delhi from February 21-23, 2020.

    Project Sahara

    Project Sahara is an outcome of one such effort by a group of students who aim to raise funds to help the underprivileged by organising online events like Treasure Hunt, Quiz and eSports.

    Republic Day Celebration

    IIIT Pune celebrated RepublicDay with Dr. Anupam Shukla, Director, IIIT Pune, hoisting the National Flag. The Students performed various programs such as drama, singing, band performances, a special blood donation camp was also organized by the IIITP.

    EBSB: A visit to IIIT Trichy

    Under EBSB student exchange program organised between IIIT Pune and IIIT Trichy. IIIT Pune students visited Trichy in Jan 2020 and IIIT Trichy students visited Pune in Feb 2020.
